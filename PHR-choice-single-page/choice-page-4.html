Choosing your GP online service

Giving you a choice of GP online services allows you to use different services to help manage your health.

I am already using GP online services

  • You can continue using your existing service - simply select it below. Or you can choose to use a new service. For this you will need the information provided by your GP practce when you first registered for GP online services.

I want to sign-up to GP online services

  • To set up a new online account you will need to ask your GP for an online services registration form.

Find out more about registering for GP online service.
