Confirming who you are

To set your data sharing preferences we need to check who you are to stop someone pretending to be you

You will be asked to complete a few short steps after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.

Before you can start you will need:

  • An email address or mobile number registered with your current GP practice
  • Access to your email or mobile phone
  • Your NHS number, if you have one. Where to find my NHS number

Important: If you have not registered your email/mobile details with your GP practice it will not be possible for you to continue with this process online. Other ways to set your preferences

Before you start you must agree to the following statement by ticking the box

Your email and mobile will not be used for any other purpose. It will never be used for marketing.

Other ways to change your data sharing options

  1. Download a form and follow the instructions
  2. Visit your GP practice and ask the receptionist for information on setting your health data sharing preferences

Useful information

  • Changing your preference will not affect your NHS treatment
  • You won't be asked for any documents or financial or medical information

If you are under 16 years old please ask a parent or guardian to help you decide

If you have previously set your preferences and would now would like to change them, you can find more information here

This only applies in England
