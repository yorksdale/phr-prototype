Our obligation

Everyone who uses health and care services should be able to trust that their personal confidential data is protected

In September 2015, the Secretary of State asked the National Data Guardian to carry out an intensive review, which recommended:

  • New data security methods
  • A method for testing compliance against these standards
  • A new consent or opt out model for data sharing in relation to patient confidential data

Keeping your online health and social care records safe and secure

Guidance is available to help you understand what an electronic health and care record is, how you can access it, who you may want to share it with, and how to perform these actions securely. This guidance was created by the Department of Health, working in collaboration with BCS, the Chartered Institute for IT, in 2013.
