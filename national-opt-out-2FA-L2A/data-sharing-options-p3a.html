Checking your identity

To opt out of your data sharing we need to match your identity with your NHS record

This will take about 5 minutes. You will be asked to complete 5 steps after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.

Before you can start you will need:

  • An email address or mobile number registered with your current GP practice
  • Access to your email or mobile phone
  • Your NHS number, if you have one. Find my NHS number

Important: if you have not previously registered your email/mobile with your GP it will not be possible for you to carry on with the opt-out process online.

Before you start you must agree to the statement by ticking the box.

Other ways to change your data sharing options

  • Download a form and follow the instructions
  • Visit your GP practice and ask the receptionist for information on opting out of sharing your data
