Making your choice

There are 4 steps to complete in order to register your preference online

  • Enter name, address and date of birth
  • Confirm how you would like to receive your one-time verification code
  • Enter your code
  • Indicate your preference

This will take about 5 minutes after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.

Your personal confidential information will only be used for purposes that benefit treatment and care. It will never be used for marketing or insurance purposes.


If you are satisfied with your personal confidential information being used for research and planning purposes you don't need to do anything, thank you for your time.


If you are not satisfied with your personal confidential information being used for research and health planning purposes you can begin registering this preference by clicking this link.
