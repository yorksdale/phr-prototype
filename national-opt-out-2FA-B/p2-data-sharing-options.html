Confirming who you are

To set your data sharing preferences we need to check who you are to stop someone pretending to be you

You will be asked to complete a few short steps after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.

Before you can start you will need:

  • An email address or mobile number registered with your current GP practice
  • Access to your email or mobile phone
  • Your NHS number, if you have one. Where to find my NHS number

Important: If you have not registered your email/mobile details with your GP practice it will not be possible for you to continue with this process online. Other ways to set your preferences
