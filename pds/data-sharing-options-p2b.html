Register your choice

There are 3 ways to set your health data preferences

  • If you have previously given your email address or mobile number to your GP practice you can continue to set your preferences online.
  • You can click here to download a form and follow the instructions
  • You can ask athe receptionist at your GP practice for the appropriate form and further information

If you are under 16 years old please ask your parents or guardian

To register your choice we need to verify your identity with your NHS record

There are 4 steps to complete in order to do this online

  • Enter name, address and date of birth
  • Confirm how you would like to receive your one-time verification code
  • Enter your code
  • Indicate your preference

This will take about 5 minutes to complete after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.
