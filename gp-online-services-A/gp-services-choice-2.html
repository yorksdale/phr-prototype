Sign into GP online services

Patients at this surgery have a choice of online services sites.

On each site, GP surgeries decide which features to make available to their patients.

Different sites sometimes allow you to do different things. For example, not all sites allow you to communicate directly with your GP. So it’s important to check that the site you choose offers the services you need.

If you’ve already joined online services

Click one of the buttons below to:

  • sign into your usual site
  • change your online services site – you’ll need the details your surgery gave you when you joined online services

If you haven’t already joined online services

1. Contact your GP surgery

Tell your surgery you want to use online services. They will need to confirm your identity, so take:

  • photo identification (for example a passport or driving licence)
  • proof of address (for example a gas or electricity bill)

The surgery will give you the information you need to sign in.

2. Sign into one of the sites below

If your surgery has sent you details to join online services, choose which site you’d like to use.

Use the Engage Consult website to:

  • Book appointments with your GP is available
  • Order repeat prescriptions is not available
  • View medical record is not available
  • View test results is not available
  • Available for mobile devices is not available
  • Communicate with GP practice is available
For more information visit the Engage Consult website

  • Order repeat prescriptions is available
